Nirmaan Organization

Nirmaan is a registered NGO that works in the areas of education, skill development, health, and community development. The organization was founded in 2005 by students of BITS Pilani.Nirmaan has impacted the lives of millions of people across India. Nirmaan has a presence in India, the USA, UK, Australia, Singapore, and UAE.Nirmaan partners with corporate, government, and philanthropic organizations
